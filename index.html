Section 4.1 · Local projections (Jordà, 2005)

Prices after a shock month vs prices after an ordinary month

Δh price = βh · Shock + γh · Shock × State + controls + ε
13 regressions · h = 0…12≈ 1,657 obs eachmonth-clustered SEsγ answers add-or-multiply directly
Causality’s heavy lift: rice prices cannot cause the weather. Macro states read as “the total effect of being in such an episode.”

The mechanism — why oil?

Everything that fights a drought runs on diesel

🚜

Water-Pumps

pump groundwater when the rain fails — diesel, litre by litre

🧪

Fertiliser

substitutes for what dry soil cannot provide — made and moved with fuel

🚚

Trucks

move grain from surplus to deficit districts — freight priced in fuel

Amplification happens when the shock and its remedy compete for the same input. A weak currency taxes everything equally — so it merely adds.

Section 5.1 · What the data represents

The polycrisis is real — but selective

1×

ordinary shocks: single-digit percentages over a year — survivable

×

drought × oil spike multiplies — +7 pp beyond the sum of parts

+

drought × weak currency merely adds — no interaction at any horizon

Pakistan 2008 & 2022-23, Bangladesh 2008 — the catastrophes are exactly the months the arithmetic stops being additive.
Section 5.2 · What a policymaker or manager should take

Watch the pairs, not the variables

Food ministry

Fuel policy = drought policy

the multiplier passes through oil — maintain strategic fuel access during a bad monsoon

Miller / importer

Hedge the currency

dollar costs against rupee income is the classic staple-market risk position

Procurement team

Red-alert months

drought + expensive oil = forward-purchasing alarm, every time

Section 5.3 · Limitations

What this paper does not claim

3 countries → we compare systems, not just numbers the 2022 flood was not “any rainy month” — intensity may be nonlinear national means hide district-level damage depreciation is not randomly assigned — read as total episode effect two minor data issues disclosed; main results unaffected
